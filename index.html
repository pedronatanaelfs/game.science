Solutions

These are the most studio-relevant game analytics deliverables—built as portfolio-grade prototypes that can evolve into production modules.

A) Retention & Cohort Intelligence

Measure player health over time (D1/D7/D30), cohort comparisons, and churn risk signals.

  • Cohort dashboards + retention curves
  • Churn prediction (interpretable baselines)
  • Automated alerts after patches/events
Best for: product & live ops

B) Level Funnel & Drop-off Diagnosis

Identify where players quit and why using progression funnels and frustration proxies.

  • Level progression funnel (tutorial → endgame)
  • Difficulty spikes (deaths/retries/time stuck)
  • Action list: what to fix next
Best for: design & balancing

C) Telemetry Pipeline & Data QA

Turn raw events into analytics-ready tables with sessionization, feature engineering, and QA checks.

  • Event schema + data contracts
  • Session reconstruction (playtime, sessions)
  • QA: missing events, outliers, drift
Best for: reliable metrics

D) Player Personas (Clustering + Explainability)

Segment players into interpretable profiles to tailor onboarding, pacing, and content.

  • Clustering + persona cards
  • Segment retention and churn risk
  • Explainability to guide actions
Best for: personalization

E) Live Ops Monitoring & Patch Impact

Operational monitoring for DAU/WAU/MAU, engagement, progression stability, and impact analysis.

  • KPI dashboard + anomaly alerts
  • Pre/post patch cohort comparison
  • Guardrails to prevent regressions
Best for: live games

F) Integrity Signals (Anomaly / Suspicious Behavior)

Generate explainable risk scores for investigation triage (cheating/bots/economy exploits).

  • Risk scoring + explainable flags
  • Outlier detection (lightweight ML)
  • Investigation dashboard prototype
Best for: multiplayer moderation
